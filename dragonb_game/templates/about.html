About Page


This is a small adventure text game based on python and the flask framework. The game is based on a very popular, and one of my favorite anime/cartoon I grew up watching as a kid. The plot summary of the game is based on a hero by the name of Goku who is trying to collect seven dragon balls, which once all collected and united have the power to make a wish. Our hero Goku sets out to find all these dragon balls and hopefully bring back his family and entire village which were exterminated by evil forces. Throughout the game he faces adversity, challenges and fights different villains in order to collect all dragon balls.
